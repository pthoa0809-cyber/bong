Skullpanda

Created by designer Xiong Miao, Skullpanda's story revolves around a girl wearing a skull mask, who has the ability to travel through time and space, bringing with her a world full of mystery and adventure. The character is designed to represent nothingness, freedom, and self-discovery through various adventures, told through different collections.

Hirono

A fictional character created by Chinese artist Lang to express the subtle emotional fluctuations of human beings. This boy often has a sulky expression, small, puffy eyes and messy hair, expressing a reserved, introverted and sometimes mischievous side. The boy symbolizes the inner world, where innocence and imagination are nurtured, and represents how each person deals with emotions such as joy, sadness, fear and difficult situations in life.
